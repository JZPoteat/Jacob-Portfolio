MyClientele

Keeping track of clients and reports can be an arduous task. Client management systems already exist, but most systems are either too expensive or do not provide enough resources for proper client management. So with a team of five developers and one Team Leader, we formulated MyClientele: a one-stop-shop for client management!

With MyClientele, employees can organize their daily schedule of clients, view/add clients with Google Maps and write reports all in one easy to use place. Administrators for each company can view a list of their employees along with their respective clients and reports.

Speak-Easy

Speak-Easy is a language learning application that assists users in learning how to speak multiple foreign languages by using spaced repetition. The app encourages the user to listen to the pronunciation of the word, then use their microphone to translate the word. The database is structured using a linked list to efficiently implement the spaced repetition algorithm. Speak-Easy currently supports multiple languages and two different learning challenges: Flashcard and Multiple Choice.

Speak-Easy was created with a team of three dedicated developers during my time at Thinkful. In the future, we will be adding support for more languages, giving the user the ability to add and delete words, and implementing other learning challenges.
